# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis – End-to-End Data Analytics Project

## 🔍 Project Overview
This project analyzes customer churn for a telecom company using SQL, Excel, and Power BI. The goal is to identify key drivers of churn, segment high-risk customers, and provide actionable business recommendations to improve customer retention.

This is a complete end-to-end analytics project covering data cleaning, transformation, analysis, visualization, and business storytelling.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
Customer churn significantly impacts revenue and growth. The objective of this project is to:

- Identify which customers are most likely to churn  
- Understand the key factors driving churn  
- Provide data-driven recommendations to reduce churn

## 📊 Key Insights

- 📉 Overall churn rate is **26.5%**, indicating a retention challenge  
- 📆 Customers on **month-to-month contracts** have the highest churn (~42%)  
- ⏳ **New customers (0–12 months)** are most likely to churn (~47–50%)  
- 💳 Customers using **electronic check** show significantly higher churn (~45%)  
- 💰 High-value customers early in their lifecycle are at the greatest risk

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Improve **customer onboarding experience** to reduce early churn  
- Encourage transition to **long-term contracts** through incentives  
- Investigate issues with **electronic check payment method**  
- Implement **targeted retention strategies** for high-value customers
